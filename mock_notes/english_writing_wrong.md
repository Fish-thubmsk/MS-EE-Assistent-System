# 错题：英语写作常见失分点

## 失分点 1：开头段套路生硬

**错误示例**：
> Nowadays, with the rapid development of society, more and more people pay attention to XXX.

**问题**：开头过于模板化，阅卷老师会扣印象分。

**改进方向**：针对图表或话题内容写具体描述性开头。

> As is vividly depicted in the picture, a student is buried in books while a smartphone lies untouched beside him, symbolizing the conflict between traditional learning and digital distraction.

## 失分点 2：段落缺乏逻辑连接词

**错误示例**：逐句罗列，没有 first/second/furthermore/however 等过渡词。

**正确示例**：
> First, excessive use of social media undermines concentration. Furthermore, it may lead to social isolation. However, if used wisely, it can be a powerful tool for learning.

## 失分点 3：结尾段不够有力

避免用 "In a word, I think..."，改用：
> In conclusion, it is imperative that we strike a balance between embracing technology and preserving the essence of traditional study habits.

## 高频词汇积累

- 现象类：prevalent / widespread / ubiquitous
- 原因类：attribute to / stem from / be rooted in
- 建议类：it is advisable to / measures should be taken to
